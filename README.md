The dataset that I have selected from the awesome-datascience is COVID-19 dataset, hosted at  https://github.com/datasets/covid-19/tree/main/data. It offers comprehensive global data on covid-19 pandemic.Every day updates on confirmed cases, deaths, and recoveries in different nations and areas are included in this collection. Researchers, data scientists, and public health officials may trace the virus's evolution, examine trends, and create scenarios for the future with the help of this valuable resource.

Creating prediction models to estimate the virus's transmission, evaluating the success of public health initiatives, and showing patterns across various geographic areas are some possible uses for this dataset. By comparing this data with other datasets, such economic indicators or migration data, it is also possible to evaluate the socio-economic effects of the pandemic.
